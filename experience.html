Here, I would like to share my experience working as a teacher's assistant in a kindergarten in 2018. After finishing SPM, I continued to look for work. While waiting for the SPM results, the nursery employer was a friend of my mother. She had offered me a job to work as an assistant in the nursery. Without thinking, I immediately accepted the offer. The salary offered to me is RM 500 per month. The nursery has 2 sessions, the morning session and the afternoon session. In the morning session, working hours start at 8.00 a.m. until 1.00 pm. The afternoon session starts at 1.00 pm and ends at 6.30 pm. I chose to work on the morning session.

As soon as I arrived at the nursery, I was instructed to greet the children who had arrived at the nursery. The number of children at that time was only 7-8. As soon as they arrived, I proceeded to bathe all the children and prepare breakfast for them. To be honest, working as a assistant in a nursery is very challenging, tests patience, and is also tiring. But I am very happy because I really like children.

I worked for 6 months. After that, I got an offer to continue studying at the pre-diploma level at UITM Seri Iskandar, Perak. After finishing my pre-diploma, I continued at the diploma level at UITM Merbok Sungai Petani, Kedah. That's all from me. Below are pictures of the children at Da Mirza Kidz Playschool.
